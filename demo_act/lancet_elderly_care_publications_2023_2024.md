## Executive Summary

This comprehensive research report synthesizes findings from The Lancet family journals and complementary research sources on elderly care and geriatrics published between 2023-2024. The research highlights critical demographic shifts, healthcare system challenges, workforce shortages, and emerging market opportunities in the global elderly care sector.

**Key Findings:**
- Global elderly care market valued at $1.15-1.93 trillion in 2024, projected to reach $2.7-3.29 trillion by 2032
- 20% of EU population now aged 65+, with 80% of global elderly projected to reside in LMICs by 2050
- Critical geriatrician shortage: only 2.4 specialists per 100,000 adults aged 65+ (declining trend)
- Lancet identifies 14 modifiable risk factors for dementia (accounting for 45% of cases)
- Major shift toward home-based care and integrated care models
