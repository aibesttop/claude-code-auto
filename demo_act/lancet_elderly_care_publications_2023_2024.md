## Research Trends and Themes

### Emerging Areas:
1. **Biological Aging Markers**: Multi-omics approaches to understanding aging
2. **Age-Friendly Environments**: Urban planning and community design for aging populations
3. **Precision Geriatrics**: Individualized treatment approaches for older adults
4. **Social Determinants**: Impact of isolation and social factors on health outcomes
5. **Long-term Outcomes**: Extended follow-up studies of interventions in older adults

### Geographic Focus:
- **China**: Significant representation in aging research
- **Europe**: Strong focus on primary care and rehabilitation
- **Global Perspective**: Multi-national studies on dementia and cardiovascular health

## Key Research Gaps Identified:
1. **Underrepresentation** of older adults in clinical trials
2. **Long-term effects** of new dementia treatments
3. **Integration** of geriatric principles in specialty care
4. **Sustainable models** for age-friendly healthcare systems

## Methodological Approaches:
- **Large-scale cohort studies** (500,000+ participants)
- **Multi-national database analyses**
- **Randomised controlled trials** with extended follow-up
- **Systematic reviews and meta-analyses**
- **Implementation science studies**

## Implications for Practice:
1. Need for improved age representation in clinical trials
2. Importance of multimodal interventions for functional decline
3. Role of social determinants in cognitive health
4. Emerging therapeutic options for dementia management
5. Value of personalized approaches in geriatric care
